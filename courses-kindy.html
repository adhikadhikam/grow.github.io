Kindeep Risk School is a kindergarten course that can be attended by younger children.

By taking English showers every day and using English through various activities, the goal is to raise children bilingually, so that they can develop their creativity and international mindsets and expand their lives to the world!

In addition to overseas culture, Japanese culture will be actively incorporated into activities to raise children with a broad perspective.

The curriculum includes English reading and writing, gymnastics, music, and math, and we are working to create an environment where you can enjoy learning naturally.

Our child focused curriculum is tailored to meet the specific needs of each students age range within our school. From the basics of number and letter recognition, reading, somersaults, cleaning up after themselves and scribbling to writing their own stories/diaries, doing cartwheels, creating a class cleaning routine and painting beautiful works of art. We continually challenge the children in each level that they are in. We strive to give our kids as many opportunities throughout the day to explore their new-found skills and create as well as harness their natural curiosity, desire to learn and will to succeed. Science Technology Engineering Math

STEM stands for Science, Technology, Engineering & Math. STEM focuses on Intellectual learning rather than Academic learning. Academic learning is the basis of memorizing, practicing, counting, reciting. Intellectual learning uses reasoning, predicting, hypothesizing and problem solving. It’s a natural learning method and uses open ended questions & brain building. STEM is a recognized and popular teaching method used in North America and at GROW we incorporate it into our classes in a fun and exciting way!
