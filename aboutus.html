Grow International Preschool

Play, Learn, Grow!

What is International School Preschool?

International preschool is a school for children whose native language is mainly English. Preschool originally means “kindergarten or nursery school” in English, but preschool in Japan is a facility that provides childcare in English. It is. I live in 100% English while at school. In addition, while living in English, you will learn a lot of things other than language, so you can learn English in a more natural way, without setting up as a study. Glow International Preschool is a fun place to learn. There is a campus in Makinohara, Meito-ku, Nagoya City, and many children come from various areas including Tenpaku, Chikusa, Showa, Moriyama, Nisshin and Nagakute.

English education in early childhood?

The time when the brain's neural circuit for stimuli from the ear is completed in the child's developmental stage is around 2 years old. If you receive a lot of stimulation during this period, it will be easier to absorb the language. In addition, by acquiring the pure listening skills unique to early childhood, you will be able to work with confidence, without having a weak awareness of future English education.

What kind of child is changing?

Glow International Preschool offers courses for different ages. There are also after-sale and Saturday courses from 6 months old baby & mom class to 5th grade. Each course has its own curriculum that inspires children. Children who continue to absorb new knowledge and grow up come to school with a sparkling smile. Based on the wishes of parents who want to grow up as adults with a global mind through English, Glow International Preschool offers lessons in a school / home mutual cooperation system in the best environment. . In addition, there is a campus in Makinohara, Meito-ku, Nagoya City, and children from nearby Chikusa-ku, Showa-ku and the suburbs also go through.

Government Certified Childcare Facility

I hear some worries that even if you become bilingual when you graduate, it will change in the post-graduation environment. Glow International Preschool has an after-course that you can attend even after graduation, so you can continue learning in an English environment. We also have a lesson course for dads and moms to speak English so that they can have English conversations with their children.

Fun And Academic

Glow International Preschool offers lessons with the goal of raising children through an English environment. In addition to short classes, we are practicing a fun academic school life to nurture a true global mind and care for children's growth. Teachers interact with children only in English, and children absorb English in the same environment as if they lived in Japan and live 100% English abroad. All activities are enjoyed so that the brain can move to the ideal bilingual state where it can passively move and communicate.

Latest Educatiional Methods

We also incorporate popular overseas learning methods on time. New ways of studying children are emerging one after another. I hope that only those that are really necessary for the growth of children will be included, and that children with a more global mind will be raised. Of course, all the events and customs that are similar to kindergartens and preschools in Japan are incorporated. There are many exercise programs, so even if you enter a Japanese elementary school, you will grow up without any problems.

Safety First

There are many things that come with children's lives. For example, there are hygiene aspects such as illness and dangers such as injury during play. At Glow International Preschool, our primary goal is to create an environment where children can attend with peace of mind and parents can leave with confidence. In addition, we teach children what they need for safety in their daily lives and guide them not to do anything dangerous.

Great Teachers

Seeing your child's day-to-day growth up close, it's a really fun job to be impressed from time to time! It is also a must-see for those who want to improve their language skills!
