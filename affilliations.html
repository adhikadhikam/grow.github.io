In working closely with the following affiliations, Grow International Preschool is continually supporting the needs of its students and families, improving the school and learning environment as well as exceeding safety and regulatory requirements.

Registered Daycare Center - Ninka Gai Hoikuen

Grow International Preschool is Registered as a Childcare provider with the Aichi prefecture as well as the City of Nagoya. Grow works regularly with city and prefecture officials to maintain and exceed provincial and national childcare standards in order to provide the best growing experience for their children. Due to Grow being a registered Childcare provider, students that attend Grow are eligible for the “Free Child Education & Childcare” Subsidy. Please click here for more information.

www.ninka-gai-hoikuen.com

TISA - Tokai International School Association

Grow International Preschool is one of the founding members of the Tokai International School Association, otherwise known as TISA. Officially founded in 2019, TISA is a group of international schools within the Tokai area which are working together to promote collaboration among member schools in order to ensure the highest level of globally minded and holistic education.

TISA aims to:

  • Share ideas and information among member schools
  • Find ways to benefit and support TISA school communities
  • Raise awareness of international education and promote high quality standards within the International School community.

You can view more information about TISA here:

www.tisa-japan.com
