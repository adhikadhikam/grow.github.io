Glow International Preschool is an international preschool located in Meito, Nagoya. Our goal is to work with your home and school to raise your children bilingual together. For children aged 1 and a half to 3 years old, we offer an infant English childcare course in a 4-hour course from Monday to Friday (2 to 2 days a week). There are many people who can attend as lessons for young children. All activities in the lessons are designed to encourage children to improve their knowledge and skills and motivate learning. In addition, using various materials and tools stimulates the five senses and fosters imagination and creative thinking. The curriculum is distributed in a well-balanced manner so as to maximize the possibilities of each child and build up a foundation for English, so that students can have fun learning the basics necessary for their future school life. Grow International Preschool Belief

Glow International Preschool in Meito-ku, Nagoya City has a lesson that attracts children's interests in an English-only environment with the motto "Play, learn and grow in English."

Through various activities, we can draw out children's imagination and curiosity, and by grasping questions such as `` Why? '', `` How? '', `` What?
